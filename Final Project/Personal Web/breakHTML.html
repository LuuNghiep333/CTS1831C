ASSEMBLY

"What is Assembly???"

The assembly system in this work is created by externally attaching parts to the pre-made humanoid body.

In addition, connectors for attaching parts are prepared throughout the base body. There are 2 types of connectors, Connectors and S-Connectors.

Parameters

Shield Durability
So-called HP. When it reaches 0, it breaks and becomes incapable of action for a certain period of time. Increased by ARMOR's "Shield Addition".
Data Weight
Simply aircraft's weight can be expressed by the overall heaviness of the aircraft [Max Data Weight].
Speed
Maximum non-boosted speed of the aircraft. Increased by THRUSTER's "thrust" and descended by increasing the weight of the aircraft.
Thrust
The total value of THRUSTER's "thrust".
Boost Thrust
The total value of BOOSTER's "Boost Thrust".
Acceleration Force
Acceleration when non-boosted. Increased by THRUSTER's "thrust" and descends by increasing the weight of the aircraft.
Boost Acceleration
Acceleration when boosting. Increased by BOOSTER's "boost thrust" and THRUSTER's "thrust", and descends by increasing the aircraft weight.
Boost Consumption
Energy consumption when boosting. Increased by BOOSTER's "Boost Thrust".
Cornering
The ability to literally turn. Increased by THRUSTER's "turning thrust" and descends by increasing the weight of the aircraft.
Energy
Maximum amount of fuel consumed when boosting. Increased by ARMOR's "energy capacity".
Energy Recovery
Amount of energy restored per second. SYSTEM's "Generator Output", increased by surplus data weight.
Energy Conversion Rate
The rate at which damage is converted to energy when hit. Increased by ARMOR's "damaged energy absorption".
Arm Data Weight
Simply Weapon's Weight.
Lock-on Distance
Lock-on distance to enemy aircraft. Long range allows for long range attacks. Increased by SYSTEM's "lock-on distance".
Lock-on Speed
Time to lock-on. If it's short, you can move quickly to attack. Descended according to SYSTEM's "calculation speed".
Generator Output
Total value of SYSTEM "generator output".
System Error Recovery
The amount of time it takes to recover from a system error. Descended according to SYSTEM's "calculation speed".

WEAPON

"What are Weapons???"

In Break Arts, it is possible to attach some kind of weapon to both arms of the machine... Or rather, it is necessary to equip a weapon that can be "fired" and "reusable".

The customization method is generally the same as attaching parts to the aircraft, but since the two conditions mentioned above must be met, the threshold is a little higher than that of the aircraft.

Parameters

Shield
The total shield value of the weapon. Added to aircraft's shield.
Data Weight
Simply weapon's total weight. Limited in range 500-4000.
Bullet Speed
The speed at which a bullet travels.
Recharge Time
Energy reference reload time. The time it takes for one bullet's worth of energy to accumulate.
Exhaust Heat Time
Reload time for thermal performance reference. Cool time until the next shot is fired after shooting.
Cooling Performance
Exhaust heat performance after shooting.
Constant Fever
Cooling performance hurdles that must be ensured at a minimum.
Fever During Attack
Amount of heat generated per shot.
Energy Capacity
Weapon's energy capacity. Weapons will not be completed if the energy consumption is less than the energy consumed when attacking.
Regular Energy Consumption
Amount of energy that is constantly consumed.
Energy Consumption when attacking
Amount of energy required to attack.
Amount of energy that is constantly consumed.
Generator Output
Weapon energy recovery. Affects recharge time and attack power per second.
Attack Power per Second
DPS calculated by heat exhaust time.
Offensive Power
Amount of damage to shields per hit when hitting an opponent.
Impact Force
Affects the knockback amount of the hit unit. Calculated from bullet weight and velocity.
Weapon Type
Shooting, Set trap, or Melee
Inductive Performance
On-Inductive, Semi-inductive, or Fully Inductive
